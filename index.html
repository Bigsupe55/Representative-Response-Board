Public response board · 119th Congress · House Oversight

Fraud-Elimination Proposal: Who Has Responded?

Sorted: longest silence first
Methodology: Each representative was contacted through their official House website contact form regarding a proposal to reduce fraud and improper payments. Timers show elapsed time since submission. Automated acknowledgment emails do not count as responses; only a substantive reply from the office flips a representative to "Responded." An automated monitor checks the submission inbox for office replies every two hours and updates statuses, filtering out acknowledgment receipts and bulk mail. The board owner reviews every automatic update and corrects errors.
